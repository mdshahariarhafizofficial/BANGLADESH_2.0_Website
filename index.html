Pillars of "Bangladesh 2.0"

Innovation

Transforming industries through advanced technology and fostering a culture of creativity to fuel national progress.

Sustainability

Advocating for eco-friendly practices, renewable energy, and green initiatives to ensure a better environment for future generations.

Infrastructure

Building modern, resilient infrastructure to connect communities and support economic growth.

Empowerment

Promoting education, equality, and opportunities to uplift all segments of society and reduce disparities.

The Vision of Future

Bangladesh 2.0 aims to redefine the nation’s trajectory by embracing cutting-edge technology, promoting inclusive growth, and ensuring sustainability. With initiatives in renewable energy, digital transformation, and robust infrastructure, the country is set to become a global model of development.

Digital Transformation

Developing a nationwide digital ecosystem to enhance connectivity and improve access to services for every citizen.

Green Energy

Investing in renewable energy, including solar and wind power, to achieve sustainability and reduce dependency on fossil fuels.

Modern Infrastructure

Constructing state-of-the-art facilities to support economic growth and enhance Bangladesh's global competitiveness.

"Bangladesh 2.0 represents a bold step forward in our journey as a nation. By embracing innovation, sustainability, and inclusive growth, we are not just building infrastructure and advancing technology—we are creating a society where every individual has the opportunity to thrive. This initiative reflects our collective dream of a prosperous, equitable, and sustainable Bangladesh. Together, we will transform challenges into opportunities and pave the way for a brighter future for generations to come."

— Dr. Muhammad Yunus

Chief Adviser of the People's Republic of Bangladesh
